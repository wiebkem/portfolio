Homeless life

Project
Gamedesign as part of the course Designing User Experiences at Chalmers University of Technology

Target user
Gaming people

Timeframe
40 hours within two weeks in 2018

Role
Interaction & UX Designer

Projectmembers
Independent

Project goal
The goal of the design was to show the endless cycle of a homeless person. It should be clear to the gamer that it is difficult for a homeless person to climb up the ladder to the "normal" people. While playing the game, the emotion Frustration should be invoked to let the gamer experience to be unable to do something.
