Construction Site Photography

As a team, consistend of 5 people, we got the background 'Photographers' and 'Construction sites'.

First of all we started with building up initial ideas with the use of the methods 'Design charette', 'Brainstorming', 'Now, How, Wow Matrix' and 'Dot Voting'. After the idea generation we decided to use the idea 'Consturction Site Photography'. To come up with a good deasign we had a semi-structured interview with a photographer and created some sketches and POP prototypes.

We tested these prototypes with a Focus group and additional user tests and performed a questionnaire as well.
Afterwards we created a digital version of our sketches.

To continue with this project it is important to make a decision in which way this app should be continued. Due to the interviews with the professional photographers we found out that they require a different set of tools than hobby photographers. At this point it is important to focus either on hobby photographers or professional photographers to reach a greater amount of people.
All in all, we learned that a project plan changes a lot. Furthermore prestudies are very important to figure out the needs of the target group and the special requirements they have. Next to that, we had difficulties to compare user test data from different iterations. This data was not comparable with each other and in a next project it is important to make them more measurable.
