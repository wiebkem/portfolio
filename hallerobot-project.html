Hallerobot - Tangible Game Design

Project
Tangible Game Design as part of the course Children's Technology at Chalmers University of Technology

Target user
Preschool children

Timeframe
3 weeks in 2017

Role
Interaction Designer, Developer, Researcher

Projectmembers
Wiebke Meyer, Jan Urbanek, Anukrit Verma and Zihua Yang

Project goal
The goal of the project was to design a game that helps teachers teach the most basic logic behind programming - putting together a sequence and executing it to reach a desired result. Children should be exposed to logic and programming at an early age, in a fun, playful and collaborative way. We collaborated with a preschool and had three workshops with a class of children aged 3-4 years old. We developed prototypes, co-designed and evaluated prototypes with the children.
